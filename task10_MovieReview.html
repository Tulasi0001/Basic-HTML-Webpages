Dark - Netflix Series

Review

"Dark" isn’t just a show, it’s a three-season migraine served in 4K — a place where your father is your son, your mother is your daughter, your aunt is your wife, and you might just be your own grandparent. Every time you think you’ve figured out who’s related to whom, another time portal opens and someone walks out calling someone else ‘Mom’ when they’re clearly younger than their ‘child.’ It’s a perfectly executed jigsaw puzzle — except every piece fits in more than one place, and sometimes the puzzle assembles itself backwards. You don’t watch Dark, you survive it — and when the credits roll, you’re left questioning time, reality, and your own family tree… just in case your grandma is actually your granddaughter."
