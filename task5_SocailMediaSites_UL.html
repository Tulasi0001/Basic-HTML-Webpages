Social Media Sites

Learning Platforms

  • LinkedIn Learning - LinkedIn is a social networking platform specifically designed for professional networking and career development. It serves as a platform for job seekers to connect with employers, share information, and find opportunities. Essentially, it's a professional version of social media, focused on career advancement, networking, and job hunting.
  • Coursera - Provides online courses, certifications, and degrees across various subjects from top universities and companies, making learning accessible from anywhere.It was founded in 2012 by Stanford University computer science professors Andrew Ng and Daphne Koller. Coursera works with universities and other organizations to offer online courses, certifications, and degrees in a variety of subjects.
  • Duolingo - A fun and interactive platform for learning languages. It uses gamification to make learning new languages engaging and easy for users of all ages. Duolingo is a popular, free online language learning platform that offers courses in over 40 languages, including Spanish, French, German, and more. It utilizes a gamified approach, breaking lessons into small, manageable units with various activities like vocabulary practice, listening, and speaking exercises. Duolingo also offers a premium option, Duolingo Plus, with ad-free learning and other perks.

Entertainment Platforms

  • Instagram - A social media platform focused on sharing photos and videos. It is a go-to app for entertainment, connecting users with celebrities, influencers, and friends.Instagram is a social media platform focused on photo and video sharing. Users can upload, edit, and share visual content, including photos and videos, and interact with others through likes, comments, and direct messaging. It also features "Stories," which are ephemeral posts that disappear after 24 hours.
  • YouTube - The world's largest video-sharing platform where users can watch, upload, and share videos on various topics like music, vlogs, tutorials, and more.YouTube is a popular video-sharing platform that allows users to upload, view, share, and comment on videos. It's a place where people can find a vast array of content, from entertainment and educational resources to user-generated videos and live streams. Essentially, it's a global platform for video content, accessible through its website and app.
